VISION, MISSION, AND CORE VALUES

VISION

To be renowned as vibrant global provider/manufacturer of electric power supplies equipment and Internet Of Thing (IOT) electronic devices, and various electronic devices with utmost consideration in environmental preservation, reduction of carbon footprints, and corporate social responsibility.

MISSION

  1. Give all people regardless of their social status the benefits of having connected globally and locally using affordable and readily available
  2. electronic devices for networking and various IOT devices
  3. Give all people regardless of their social status the benefits of having affordable, durable, and cost efficient electronic devices for household use and/or personal use
  4. Create job for people around the globe as part of our corporate social responsibility
  5. Give new generation a conducive earth to live with their love ones by developing products that are eco-friendly

CORE VALUES

CREATE stands for:
Customer first: 'customer first' core value means always doing what's best for the customer even if it means more work for our service experts.
Results focused: succeed by the results our customers see with our product.
Easy to use: We favor a user-friendly and eco-friendly approach in everything we produce.
Always human: being supportive and fair to each other and to hold ourselves responsible for our actions and accept responsibility for them.
Transparent and inclusive: Open communication is critical to our success.
Extraordinary: this means we favor maximizing quality and meeting deadlines at same time at most cost efficient way in order to give our customer the edge of satisfaction.
HEPI motto: is to create customer satisfaction beyond their expectation in the least possible effect on environment. At the same time, fulfill our commitment to give our employees a better life and a well-balance lifestyle between work and home.
